Company Process Guide

How we work — workflows, roles, and processes. Internal use only.
🎓 Intern Recruitment Process
1
Find Contacts Che
Che researches universities and identifies professors in animation, design, VFX, and motion graphics departments. Contacts are logged in the Intern Outreach Sheet by state.
Owner: Che (che@austinvisuals.com)
2
Initial Outreach Che Tony
Che (or Tony) emails professors to ask if they're looking to place interns. Tone is warm and natural — not a mass blast.
Roles sought: Motion Graphics, 2D Animation, VFX, Editor/Motion Designer
3
Professor Responds — Send Job Description Che Matt
If a professor expresses interest, send them the internship description. Che handles this when possible; if she needs help with tone or detail, she forwards to Matt.
4
Student Introduction Professor
The professor identifies an interested student and makes an email introduction connecting them to Austin Visuals.
5
Intro Call Matt
Matt schedules and conducts an intro call with the student to discuss the internship, expectations, and fit.
Owner: Matt Winters (matt@austinvisuals.com)
6
Intern Starts Matt Team
Intern is onboarded, introduced to the team, assigned to a project, and given access to tools they need.
📋 Project Lifecycle
1
Lead / Prospect Matt
Client inquiry received. Matt assesses fit, responds, and if promising, moves to proposal stage. Prospect folder created in Dropbox.
2
Proposal & NDA Matt Tony
Proposal sent to client. NDA signed if required. Tony logs NDA status in project registry.
3
Project Start + 50% Deposit Curtis/Jennifer
Client accepts and pays 50% deposit. Dropbox client folder created with 7 subfolders. Artist assigned.
4
Production Artist Matt/Shelly
Storyboards, concept images, animation, revisions. Artist uploads to Dropbox Renders folder. Status updates logged to project registry.
5
25% Milestone Billing Curtis/Jennifer
At midpoint milestone, 25% invoice sent. Nancy tracks in status sheet.
6
Final Delivery + 25% Final Invoice Curtis/Jennifer
Client approves final. Final 25% invoiced and collected. Files delivered.
7
Collect & Archive Artist Tony
Artist uploads final project files to Dropbox. Tony moves project to Collect/Archive status. Google review survey sent to client.
8
Portfolio Upload David
If no NDA, David uploads final video to YouTube/Vimeo, creates portfolio page on austinvisuals.com. Links logged to Best Of sheet.
⚡ Tony's Role in the Process
•
File Routing
Automatically routes emailed attachments to the correct Dropbox project folder (Renders, Client Reference, or Uploads staging).
•
Project Registry
Maintains a live dashboard of all projects with status, assigned artist, % complete, due dates, and next steps.
•
Best Of Sheet Sync
When a new project is created, automatically adds a row to the AV Best Work Samples sheet with the Dropbox folder link.
