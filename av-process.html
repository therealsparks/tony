Company Process Guide

Distilled from the 2023–2026 Austin Visuals playbooks: sales, production, roles, and growth priorities. Internal use only.
1
Inbound triage Matt Tony
All leads (web form, phone, referrals, ads) are acknowledged within one hour. Tony logs the inquiry in Pipedrive + Project Registry and attaches any intake notes.
2
Discovery call & notes Matt Nancy
Matt runs the first call, captures timeline/budget/use case, and flags creative requirements. Nancy joins as needed for high-touch prospects.
3
Proposal sprint Nancy Sales Ops
Nancy drafts treatment, scope, and milestone billing (standard 50% / 25% / 25%). Matt edits tone/pricing, Tony prepares a live-present deck if needed.
4
Live delivery & follow-up Matt
Proposal is presented live (Zoom/Meet). NDA handled when required. Next steps + decision date captured in Pipedrive; Tony sets auto-reminders.
5
Sales→Production handoff Nancy Project Mgmt
Upon deposit, Nancy schedules internal kickoff, shares Dropbox link, drops all brief materials into "Client Reference," and assigns the project owner + artist.
1
Brief + Creative Blueprint Nancy
Kickoff call, goals, audience, tone. Deliverables logged in Project Registry. For animation, lock script + mood boards before art begins.
2
Previsualization Storyboard / Animatic
Storyboards, style frames, and VO casting. Animatics include temp VO + timing. Nothing moves to production without sign-off.
3
Build + Animation Artists Tony
Scene builds, animation passes, lighting/compositing. Artists upload work-in-progress renders to Dropbox ➜ Renders. Tony tags versions and updates status dashboards.
4
Client review (watermarked) Matt
Watermarked previews delivered with Loom notes. Standard: 2 structured revision rounds unless scope expanded. Feedback logged in Tracker.
5
Final polish + delivery Team
After approvals and final 25% payment, un-watermarked masters + source files go into "Project Files" + "Uploads" for client delivery.
1
Milestone cadence
Deposit 50% to start, 25% at midpoint (after storyboard/animatic approval), 25% at final delivery. Exceptions require Matt approval.
2
Weekly status pulse
Nancy (or assigned PM) sends Friday recap: progress, blockers, next week plan. Tony auto-drafts based on project registry notes.
3
Gateway communications
Tony watches gateway uptime and emails processed. Alerts only fire if a handler fails or client assets arrive.
4
Post-project follow-up
Send review survey + referral incentive (5% cash / $500 flat). Add approved work to Best Work Samples + YT/Vimeo unless NDA prohibits.
1
Seven-folder structure
Every project gets: Client / Art / Client Reference / Project Files / Renders / Scripts / Uploads. Do not add or rename folders.
2
Naming + retention
Files labeled `ProjectName_V#_Description.ext`. Artists keep local copies for 60 days minimum. No client file deletions without Matt approval.
3
Uploads & staging
Client-provided assets land in Uploads ➜ Tony routes to proper subfolder after tagging. Email attachments auto-sort within 30 seconds.
4
Archive hygiene
After final delivery, Tony marks project as "Collected" and moves copies to Archive. Artists confirm no local exclusives remain.
